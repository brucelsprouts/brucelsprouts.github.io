Smokin' Joe

July 25, 2024 - August 7, 2024

This was a rip reel project I made during my internship at AMG.

Description

During my internship at AMG, I worked on a rip reel project where I collaborated with employees and the head of the company to create a trailer for a film that was being proposed to larger studios. My primary role was to develop the front-end components of the trailer. I was responsible for designing the user interface, creating visual assets in Photoshop, and implementing the visuals using Adobe Premiere Pro. Additionally, I assisted with some back-end tasks, such as ensuring smooth transitions and synchronizing audio with video. This project required close collaboration with the team to meet the high standards expected by potential studio partners. Because I was working on-site, I had to use a different computer and therefore didn't have access to all my tools. The Countdown project is a better example of what I can achieve when I have access to all my resources. This experience provided valuable insights into the film industry and the process of pitching projects to major studios.
